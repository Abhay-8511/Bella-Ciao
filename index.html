About Bella Ciao

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi Family, Bella Ciao has been serving authentic Itialian cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients has been made us a beloved destination for food lovers.

Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, using recipes passed down though generation of italian chefs.
